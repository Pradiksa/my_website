01 About PROFILE.LOG

Pradiksa is a software engineer with three and a half years of experience designing, building, and deploying software across desktop, embedded, and web domains. Her core strength is bridging low-level, real-time systems with usable interfaces — reading device data over MQTT, Modbus, or BACnet, and rendering it as something a human can act on.

She works comfortably across the stack: Python and PyQt5 for desktop tools, C++ and Qt for high-performance embedded GUIs, Flask for backend APIs, and React / React Native / Flutter on the front end. She's equally at home optimizing a point-cloud renderer as she is shipping a full e-commerce platform.
